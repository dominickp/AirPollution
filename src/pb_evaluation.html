What did you learn about the data by using your visualizations?

One of the most interesting things we learned, which only became apparent when interacting with our own visualization, was how little pollution data exists for some areas of the world. Once we had all of our particulate matter data plotted on the globe map, you can easily see how regions like Africa, Antarctica, and huge swaths of South America are left empty.

In general, I had previously thought China was the worst when it came to air pollution. But that crown is really reserved for India and other parts of the Middle East who are in their own league of PM 2.5 pollution.
